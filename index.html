Minecraft Bedrock Edition PvP Tier Rankings

Similar to MCTIERS, based on Bedrock 1.21 combat mechanics and 1v1 performance

HT1 Elite players: Top performers, master all mechanics, define the current meta, rarely make mistakes.
LT1 Excellent players: Strong fundamentals, consistent results, only small gaps compared to HT1 players.
HT2 Solid players: Good skill level, can compete well but are outmatched by higher tiers in long matches.
LT2 Intermediate players: Still learning and improving, results can be inconsistent from match to match.
HT3 Beginner players: Still mastering basic controls and strategies, limited experience in competitive play.
LT3 New players: Just starting out, learning core concepts and how the game works.
